Features

Forged In Darkness

Every mechanic crafted to deliver a visceral, skill-defining combat experience unlike anything else on the platform.

Deep Combat

Light swings, slams, blocks, and parries weave into infinite combos. Read your opponent, punish mistakes.

Abyssal Abilities

Wind Gusts, Flickering Swords, and the Abysmal Dark Hole — each ability a tool of devastation.

Timing Parry

Master the perfect parry window to turn the tide. Skill, not luck, decides the victor.

Momentum Dash

Directional dashing with Q + WASD creates fluid movement. Position is everything in the abyss.

Visceral Atmosphere

Cinematic lighting, abyssal effects, and a moody soundscape draw you into the void.

Endless Mastery

No ceiling. Only you and the depths. Become legend — or be forgotten in the dark.
